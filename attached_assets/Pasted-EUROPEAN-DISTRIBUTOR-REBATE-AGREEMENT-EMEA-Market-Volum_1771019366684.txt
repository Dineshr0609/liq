3. PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM 
This Agreement establishes the commercial rebate framework designed to: 
• Incentivize distributor sales growth across EMEA 
• Reward higher Net Sales performance 
• Maintain structured tier progression 
• Support regional channel expansion 
Rebates are strictly governed by contract terms, qualifiers, and eligibility validation rules. 

 
4. PROGRAM ELIGIBILITY 
Eligibility requires: 
• Active partner assignment 
• Sales within authorized territories 
• Sales through approved channels 
• Compliance with reporting obligations 
• Valid rebate claims submission 
• Proof of performance (where required) 
Only eligible Net Sales transactions qualify.

Common Term Conditions – Tiered Volume Rebates 
All Tiered Volume Rebates described above: 
• Currency: EUR 
• Payment Timing: Quarterly 
• Payment Method: Wire 
• Requires Claim: TRUE 
• Claim Deadline: 60 Days 
• Proof of Performance: TRUE 
 
Calculation Methodology 
Rebate Amount = Eligible Net Sales × Applicable Tier Rate 
Tier determination is based on cumulative eligible Net Sales Value per calendar quarter. 
 
Tier Application Logic 
Tiers are applied progressively based on cumulative eligible Net Sales per calendar quarter. 
• The highest achieved tier applies to eligible quarterly sales 
• Tier eligibility resets each quarter unless specified otherwise 
• All validation rules must pass for payout eligibility 
 
7. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS 
Partners must submit: 

• Quarterly Net Sales reports 
• Territory & channel breakdown 
• Returns / adjustments 
• Claim documentation 
• Supporting financial records 
Failure to submit compliant reports may delay payments. 
 
8. CLAIM VALIDATION 
All rebates subject to: 
• Sales eligibility verification 
• Channel compliance validation 
• Territory authorization checks 
• Tier threshold confirmation 
• Proof-of-performance review 
 
9. AUDIT AND COMPLIANCE 
Tech Sound Audio reserves the right to audit: 
• Sales records 
• Rebate calculations 
• Claims documentation 
• Financial adjustments 
Material discrepancies may result in rebate recovery. 
 
10. TERMINATION 
This Agreement may be terminated for:

Upon termination, unpaid rebates may be voided.
