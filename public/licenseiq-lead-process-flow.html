5. Verification Form Details

The self-service form that leads fill out after clicking the email link.

Form URL Pattern

  • URL: /verify/<unique-token>
  • Token: 32-byte crypto random hex string
  • One-time use — shows "Already Verified" after submission
  • Invalid/expired tokens show error page

Form Fields (5 Questions)

  • 1. Company Website URL — Optional text input
  • 2. Your Role / Job Title — Required text input
  • 3. Number of Contracts — Required dropdown (1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-500, 500+)
  • 4. Goals with LicenseIQ — Required textarea
  • 5. How did you hear about us? — Required dropdown (LinkedIn, Google, Referral, etc.)

Pre-filled Information (Read-only)

  • Name — from the original signup
  • Email — from the original signup
  • Company — from the original signup

After Submission

  • Data stored as JSONB in verification_data column
  • Timestamp saved in verified_at
  • Lead status auto-updated to "Verified"
  • Shows success page: "Verification Complete"
  • Repeat visits show: "Already Verified"

11. After Account Creation — What the New User Can Do

The new Company Admin user has full control over their company within the platform.

Immediate Access

  • Log in with username + temporary password
  • Automatically lands in their company context
  • Company visible in the top-right context switcher
  • Only sees data belonging to their company

Company Admin Capabilities

  • Create additional users for their company
  • Assign roles: admin, editor, viewer, auditor
  • Create business units and locations
  • Upload and process contracts
  • Configure calculation rules

Data Isolation

  • Multi-tenant: each company's data is isolated
  • Organization context filters all queries
  • Cannot access other companies' data
  • System admins can see all companies

Where Their Company Appears

  • Enterprise Master — in the company hierarchy
  • User Management — their users listed
  • Context Switcher — top-right dropdown
  • Contracts — filtered to their company
