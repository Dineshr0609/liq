# Rebate Contract Rules - Vontair & DistributorOne

## Contract Overview
- **Effective Date**: February 1, 2025
- **Parties**: Vontair Mobility Systems, Inc. & DistributorOne Fuel Services Inc.
- **Term**: 2 years initial, auto-renews for 1-year periods

---

## CORE REBATE RULES

### Rule 1: Quarterly Tier-Based Rebates
**Trigger**: End of each calendar quarter  
**Calculation Basis**: Total Quarterly Net Purchases (non-marginal)

| Tier | Quarterly Net Purchases (USD) | Rebate % |
|------|------------------------------|----------|
| 1    | $0 - $1,000,000             | 2%       |
| 2    | $1,000,001 - $5,000,000     | 4%       |
| 3    | $5,000,001+                 | 6%       |

**Logic**:
```
IF Quarterly_Net_Purchases <= 1,000,000 THEN
    Rebate = Quarterly_Net_Purchases × 0.02
ELSE IF Quarterly_Net_Purchases <= 5,000,000 THEN
    Rebate = Quarterly_Net_Purchases × 0.04
ELSE
    Rebate = Quarterly_Net_Purchases × 0.06
```

**Important Notes**:
- Net Purchases = Gross purchases minus tax, credits, and returns
- Rebate applies to ALL purchases in the quarter (not just amount above tier threshold)

---

### Rule 2: Launch Incentive (Special Program)
**Active Period**: First 2 quarters from February 1, 2025 (Q1 & Q2 2025)  
**Product**: Vontair Analytics Module only  
**Additional Rebate**: +1% on top of standard tier rebate

**Logic**:
```
IF Quarter IN [Q1_2025, Q2_2025] AND Product = "Vontair Analytics Module" THEN
    Additional_Rebate = Analytics_Module_Purchases × 0.01
    Total_Rebate = Standard_Tier_Rebate + Additional_Rebate
```

---

### Rule 3: Growth Accelerator (Annual Bonus)
**Trigger**: End of contract year  
**Threshold**: Annual Net Purchases > $12,000,000  
**Bonus**: Additional 2% on purchases exceeding $10,000,000

**Logic**:
```
IF Annual_Net_Purchases > 12,000,000 THEN
    Bonus_Eligible_Amount = Annual_Net_Purchases - 10,000,000
    Year_End_Bonus = Bonus_Eligible_Amount × 0.02
ELSE
    Year_End_Bonus = 0
```

**Example**:
- Annual purchases = $13,000,000
- Bonus eligible amount = $13M - $10M = $3M
- Year-end bonus = $3M × 2% = $60,000

## PAYMENT & REPORTING RULES

### Rule 4: Rebate Statement Generation
**Timeline**: Within 30 days after quarter-end  
**Required Information**:
- Net Purchases by month
- Net Purchases by product family
- Applicable rebate tier
- Rebate percentage applied
- Total rebate amount due

### Rule 5: Rebate Payment
**Timeline**: Within 45 days after quarter-end  
**Payment Method**: Credit memo OR wire transfer  
**Dependencies**: Payment contingent on complete resale data submission

---

## DATA & COMPLIANCE RULES

### Rule 6: Distributor Data Submission
**Deadline**: Within 15 days after quarter-end  
**Required Data**:
- Point-of-sale (POS) / resale data
- End customer information
- Product SKU details
- Quantity sold

**Critical**: Rebates will NOT be paid without complete data submission

### Rule 7: Audit Rights
**Frequency**: Once per contract year maximum  
**Notice Required**: 30 days written notice  
**Scope**: Distributor's resale records  
**Remediation**: Over-credited rebates must be reimbursed or offset in future periods

---

## TERMINATION RULES

### Rule 8: Contract Renewal
**Auto-Renewal**: Automatic 1-year extensions  
**Opt-Out**: Written non-renewal notice required 60 days before term end

### Rule 9: Termination for Breach
**Cure Period**: 30 days to remedy breach  
**Rebate Treatment**: Accrued but unpaid rebates honored up to termination date

## CALCULATION EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Standard Quarterly Rebate
**Scenario**: Q1 2025 Net Purchases = $3,500,000

**Calculation**:
- Tier 2 applies (4% rebate)
- Rebate = $3,500,000 × 0.04 = $140,000

---

### Example 2: Launch Incentive
**Scenario**: Q1 2025  
- Total Net Purchases = $2,000,000
- Analytics Module Purchases = $500,000

**Calculation**:
- Standard rebate (Tier 2): $2,000,000 × 0.04 = $80,000
- Launch incentive: $500,000 × 0.01 = $5,000
- **Total Q1 Rebate = $85,000**

---

### Example 3: Full Year with Growth Accelerator
**Scenario**: Annual purchases = $15,000,000  
- Q1: $3M (Tier 2, 4%)
- Q2: $4M (Tier 2, 4%)
- Q3: $6M (Tier 3, 6%)
- Q4: $2M (Tier 2, 4%)

**Calculation**:
- Q1 rebate: $3M × 0.04 = $120,000
- Q2 rebate: $4M × 0.04 = $160,000
- Q3 rebate: $6M × 0.06 = $360,000
- Q4 rebate: $2M × 0.04 = $80,000
- **Quarterly rebates total = $720,000**

- Growth Accelerator: ($15M - $10M) × 0.02 = $100,000
- **GRAND TOTAL REBATES = $820,000**
