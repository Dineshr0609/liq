# Sample Contract Creation Prompts for liQ AI

Use these prompts in the liQ AI Agent tab on the Financial Control Center. Click the chat, paste any prompt below, and hit Enter to create a contract with rules automatically.

After creating each contract, try the sample Q&A questions listed below it to test the RAG system.

---

## 1. Distributor Reseller Agreement — Super Lenses

**Create Prompt:**
```
Create a contract "Distributor Reseller Agreement" between Super Lenses Company and North West Distribution effective 03/03/2026 to 03/03/2029. Products: Headphones (SKU: SL-HP, MAP: $80 USD / $85 CAD) and Soundbars (SKU: SL-SB, MAP: $150 USD / $160 CAD). Territory: US and Canada. Volume rebate tiers: Tier 1 (0-1000 units) 2% of Net Sales, Tier 2 (1001-5000 units) 5% of Net Sales, Tier 3 (5001+ units) 8% of Net Sales. Rebates calculated retrospectively per quarter. Payment net 30 days.
```

**Sample Questions to Ask After Creating:**
- What rebate tiers apply to North West Distribution?
- What is the MAP price for Super Lenses Headphones in Canada?
- When does the Distributor Reseller Agreement expire?

## Cross-Contract Questions (Ask After Creating Multiple Contracts)

These questions work best after you've created several contracts above:

- What are all my active contracts with TechSound Audio Inc?
- Which contracts cover the European territory?
- Compare the license fee rates across all my distribution contracts.
- Which contracts have a minimum annual guarantee over $50,000?
- What are the payment terms across all my active agreements?
- Which contracts include exclusive distribution rights?
- List all contracts that cover Headphones as a product category.
- What is my total minimum guarantee obligation across all contracts?
- Which contracts expire before the end of 2027?

## Tips for Writing Your Own Prompts

- **Start with** "Create a contract" so liQ AI knows you want to generate a contract
- **Include the contract name** in quotes for clarity
- **Name both parties** — the manufacturer/licensor and the distributor/licensee
- **Specify dates** in MM/DD/YYYY format
- **List products** with SKUs if you have them
- **Define territories** — be specific about countries or regions
- **Describe fee structure** — percentages, tiers, flat fees, minimums
- **Mention payment terms** — frequency, net days, currency
- **Add special conditions** — exclusivity, MAP policy, audit rights, bonuses
