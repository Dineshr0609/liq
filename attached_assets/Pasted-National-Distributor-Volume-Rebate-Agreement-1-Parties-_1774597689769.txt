National Distributor Volume Rebate Agreement 
1. Parties 
This	Agreement	is	entered	into	between:	
Company:	Tech	Sound	Audio	("Manufacturer")	
Distributor:	National	Distributor	("Distributor")	
2. Agreement Type 
Contract	Type:	Rebate	
Contract	Category:	Channel	Incentive	
Program	Type:	Standard	National	Distributor	Program	
3. Effective Dates 
Effective	Date:	January	1,	2026	
Term:	3	years	unless	terminated	by	either	party	with	60	days	written	notice.	
4. Territory 
This	agreement	applies	to	the	following	territories:	
United	States	(USD)	
Canada	(CAD)	
Mexico	(MXN)	
5. Eligible Products 
All	products	listed	below	are	eligible	for	rebate	calculations:	
	
SoundPro	Wireless	Headphones	-	Black	(TS-WH-PRO-001-BLK)	
SoundPro	Wireless	Headphones	-	White	(TS-WH-PRO-001-WHT)	
SoundPro	Wireless	Headphones	-	Silver	(TS-WH-PRO-001-SLV)	
PremiumSound	Max	-	Black	(TS-WH-PRM-002-BLK)	
PremiumSound	Max	-	White	(TS-WH-PRM-002-WHT)	
EssentialSound	Wireless	-	Black	(TS-WH-ESS-003-BLK)	

EssentialSound	Wireless	-	White	(TS-WH-ESS-003-WHT)	
Studio	Wired	Pro	-	Black	(TS-WD-STD-001-BLK)	
6. Rebate Structure 
Distributor	earns	quarterly	volume	rebates	based	on	total	eligible	units	purchased.	
	
Tier	1:	0	–	1,000	units	→	2%	rebate	
Tier	2:	1,001	–	5,000	units	→	5%	rebate	
Tier	3:	5,001+	units	→	8%	rebate	
	
Rebate	percentage	applies	to	Net	Sales	for	eligible	products.	
7. Calculation Period 
Rebates	are	calculated	quarterly	based	on	total	units	purchased	during	the	quarter.	
8. Eligible Sales 
Eligible	sales	include	distributor	purchases	of	listed	products	within	the	territory.	
	
The	following	are	excluded:	
Returned	products	
Warranty	replacements	
Promotional	sample	units	
9. Reporting Requirement 
Distributor	must	provide	quarterly	sales	data	within	10	days	after	quarter	end	including:	
SKU	
Units	Sold	
Net	Sales	Amount	
Territory	
10. Rebate Payment 
Manufacturer	will	calculate	rebates	and	issue	payment	within	30	days	after	quarter	end.	

11. Adjustments 
Returns	or	credits	issued	after	quarter	close	may	adjust	the	original	rebate	calculation.	
Any	overpaid	rebates	will	be	offset	against	future	rebates.	
12. Termination 
Upon	termination,	any	earned	but	unpaid	rebates	will	be	calculated	and	paid	within	30	
days.
