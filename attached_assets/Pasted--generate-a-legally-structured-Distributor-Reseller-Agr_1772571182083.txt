BUSINESS LOGIC REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------------------

1) Volume-Based Quarterly Rebate (Retrospective)

The contract must include:

- Quarterly rebate calculation
- Based on cumulative quarterly net sales volume
- Retrospective adjustment logic
- Rebate tiers:

Example Default Tiers (Editable in UI):
Tier 1: 0 – 1,000 units → 2%
Tier 2: 1,001 – 5,000 units → 5%
Tier 3: 5,001+ units → 8%

Language must specify:
"Rebates shall be calculated retrospectively based on total cumulative quarterly purchases and applied to all eligible units within that quarter."

Rebates are paid within 45 days after quarter end.

----------------------------------------------------

2) Minimum Resale Price (Price Floor Enforcement)

Contract must include:

- Distributor shall not sell below agreed Minimum Advertised Price (MAP)
- MAP defined per product and per territory
- Breach consequences:
  - First violation: Written warning
  - Second violation: Suspension of rebates
  - Third violation: Termination rights

Example Base Floor Pricing (Editable):
Headphones:
  - US: $80
  - Canada: $85
Soundbars:
  - US: $150
  - Canada: $160
